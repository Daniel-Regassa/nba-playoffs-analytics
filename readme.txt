Web Scrapper:
    To use, delete playoffs.csv  and run all cells in Web_Scrapper
    
CSV_Format:
    Year - Year of Playoff Series
    Conference - Eastern or Western
    Round - Round of Playoffs
    Team1 - Team with homecourt advantage
    Seed1 - Seed of Team1 in regular season in their conference
    Odds1 - Odds of Team1 to win before series, given in American odds format
    Team2 - Team without homecourt advantage
    Seed2 - Seed of Team2 in regular season in their conference
    Odds2 - Odds of Team2 to win before series, given in American odds format
    WinningTeam - Team that won the series
    Result - Final Score of playoff series
